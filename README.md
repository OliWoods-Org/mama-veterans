> **22 veterans die by suicide every day.** Many don't know what benefits they've earned. The VA system is deliberately complex. Navigating it alone is a full-time job.

## The Solution

8 specialized agents covering every aspect of veteran life — benefits, healthcare, housing, employment, education, mental health, legal, and family services. MANDATORY Veterans Crisis Line handoff.

## Agents

**BenefitsNavigator** — VA eligibility, claim filing, appeals guidance
**HealthcareConnector** — VA hospital finder, telehealth, appointment scheduling
**HousingAssistant** — HUD-VASH vouchers, transitional housing, SSVF
**EmploymentCoach** — MOS-to-civilian skills translation, veteran-preference jobs
**EducationAdvisor** — GI Bill, VET TEC, scholarship finder
**MentalHealthSupport** — PTSD resources, peer support, VCL connection
**LegalAid** — Discharge upgrades, VA claim appeals
**FamilyServices** — Military family support, childcare, spouse employment

## Safety

**MANDATORY: Veterans Crisis Line — 988 then press 1.** Any crisis signal from a veteran triggers immediate handoff. Cannot be disabled.

## Data Sources

VA.gov APIs, DoD SkillBridge, state veteran affairs offices
